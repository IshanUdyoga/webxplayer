About WebXPlayer

A powerful free HTML5 video player for streaming media from URLs and playing local files.

What is WebXPlayer?

WebXPlayer is a versatile HTML5-based media player designed to provide a seamless viewing experience for both online streams and local media files. Our goal is to offer a comprehensive yet easy-to-use solution for all your video playback needs, directly in your web browser without requiring any downloads or installations.

Whether you want to watch videos from online sources by simply pasting a URL, or play media files from your device with full subtitle support, WebXPlayer has you covered with its intuitive interface and powerful features.

Key Features

URL Streaming

Stream videos directly from URLs with support for various protocols including HTTP, HLS, and DASH.

Local Media Playback

Play video files from your device with support for all common formats (MP4, WebM, MKV, and more).

Subtitle Support

Display subtitles with customizable appearance, position, timing, and format support (SRT, VTT, SSA).

Playlist Management

Create and manage playlists of both online streams and local media for continuous playback.

URL History

Keep track of previously streamed URLs for quick access to your favorite content.

Advanced Controls

Enjoy precise control with playback speed adjustment, volume control, and keyboard shortcuts.

Why Choose WebXPlayer?

Universal Compatibility

WebXPlayer works on any modern browser across various devices including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones, providing a consistent experience regardless of your platform.

Privacy-Focused

Your media files and streams are processed locally in your browser. We don't collect or store any of your media data, ensuring complete privacy while you enjoy your content.

No Installation Required

As a web-based solution, WebXPlayer requires no downloads or installations. Simply visit our website and start playing your media instantly.

Completely Free

WebXPlayer is offered completely free of charge, with no advertisements or premium features locked behind paywalls.

Supported Formats

WebXPlayer supports a wide range of video and audio formats:

  • Video: MP4, WebM, MKV, AVI, MOV, FLV
  • Audio: MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC
  • Streaming: HLS (.m3u8), DASH (.mpd), HTTP Progressive
  • Subtitles: SRT, WebVTT, SSA/ASS

Browser compatibility for specific formats may vary based on the built-in codecs of your browser.

How to Use

Using WebXPlayer is simple:

  1. To stream from a URL: Paste the video URL in the input field and click Play.
  2. To play local files: Click "Add Media" and select files from your device.
  3. To add subtitles: Click the subtitle button in the player controls and select a subtitle file.
  4. To manage playback: Use the intuitive controls or keyboard shortcuts.

For the best experience, we recommend using modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari.
